As millions of guests' vacation with Carnival every year, we are "The World's Most Popular Cruise Line". With 24 ships in its fleet, 20 departure ports across the United States, and two to 15-day cruises to The Bahamas, the Caribbean, Mexico, Canada, New England, Alaska, Hawaii, Bermuda, Europe, and Australia....Carnival has a cruise for everyone! For over 49 years, Carnival has been one of the most affordable and family-friendly cruise lines in the industry, and now, more than ever, the health and safety of passengers is Carnival Cruise Line's top priority. 

Carnival is ready to cruise again!    

   Carnival specializes in budget-friendly fun, but it also takes pride in also offering a stress-free and luxurious vacation. Cruising offers stress-free vacation planning because it is an all-inclusive option, including the travel itinerary, dining, and entertainment as part of the cruise package. Carnival also offers dining options that are world class, and have partnerships with celebrity chefs Guy Fieri and Emeril Lagasse. Relaxing spa treatments at sea, in Spa Carnival, offer a variety of spa services to relax and rejuvenate. The large ships also offer small, intimate, quiet places to escape the crowds, such as the adult only Serenity Deck.

Carnival's highest responsibility and top priorities are compliance, environmental protection, the communities we visit, and the health, safety and well-being of all guests, crew, and shoreside employees. Highlights of Carnival's newly-adopted health protocols include testing of all passengers and crew for COVID-19 prior to departure, mandatory mask-wearing for all passengers and crew, improved ventilation to increase fresh air onboard, and onboard medical facilities for isolation and quarantine, and the ability to make arrangements with private providers at various ports of call. Following CDC guidelines, Carnival will also be incorporating vaccination plans for all crew members.





Persuasion Theory

With health and safety concerns at the forefront of Audience A's purchasing decisions, and the reliance on the expertise and trustworthiness of the source delivering the persuasive message, central processing is most appropriate. The message of safety is also congruent to this audiences' attitudes and beliefs to exceed health and safety standards. Audience A will pay very close attention to the messaging information and will carefully examine the message's arguments, according to O'Keefe's Elaboration Likelihood Model. The audience will carefully consider the message's claims and arguments, and will be guided by the expertise of the communicator, which is in the form of a safe and clean ship.

Target Audience

Members of Audience A are predominantly white/Caucasian, over the age of 45 and are married couples who are well-educated with both college graduates and postgraduates. The average household income would be in the range of $90,000 or more, with the travelers to either be employed full-time, or retired. Geographic location of this audience is not relevant as they would have the means to travel to different ports regardless of where they reside in the United States. Members of Audience A are frequent travelers, have loyalty to Carnival Cruise Lines and have taken many cruise vacations with Carnival over the years.        

The Design of this Page

The design choice, to use different shades of blue, is to be reminiscent of a clear blue sky and ocean water, with the white backgrounds as a reminder of clouds and the white caps of ocean waves. The color blue is viewed as a non-threatening color and evokes feelings of calmness or serenity, which is the focus of the campaign. The repetition of the same font throughout and the use of images that are clean, clear, and without human clutter, reflects Carnival's preparedness across their entire fleet of ships, and the clean lines emphasize cleanliness, health, and safety.
